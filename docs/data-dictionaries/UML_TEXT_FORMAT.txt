## 2.1.2 Conditional System Business Rules (Include / Extend Contracts)

### Standard Inclusions (`<<include>>`)
*   **Log Fuel Transaction** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* `View Asset Detail` first.
*   **Create Job Card** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* An active `Breakdown Report` to exist.
*   **Add Parts to Job Card** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* `Job Card` status to be active and open.
*   **Close Job Card** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* Mechanics to `Update Job Card Status` first.
*   **Approve Transfer** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* A backend `Compliance Check` to pass successfully.
*   **Dispatch Asset** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* A `Pre-Departure Inspection` to be completed.
*   **Confirm Arrival** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* A `Post-Arrival Inspection` to be verified.
*   **Close Incident** $\rightarrow$ *Requires:* The investigator to have `Root Cause` details recorded first.

### Functional Extensions (`<<extend>>`)
*   **Record Repair Warranty** $\rightarrow$ *Extends:* `Close Job Card — Supervisor Approval` *(Triggered optionally upon contract completion)*.
*   **Override High-Risk Driver Assignment** $\rightarrow$ *Extends:* `Assign Driver to Asset` *(Triggered optionally to bypass risk compliance warning panels)*.
*   **Enter Police Report Number** $\rightarrow$ *Extends:* `File Incident Report` *(Triggered optionally for on-road vehicle accidents)*.
